Indie Authors

Why Indie Authors?

We love our small pub and indie authors! Running an independent bookstore is a tough business. At the start of this venture, we tried carrying books on consignment, but we quickly learned that this was outside of our comfort zone. In talking to other bookstores, we learned a strategy that has worked for us: Indie Author Night! This is our night to celebrate local and indie authors, which we generally host a few times a year. We bring in 10-20 authors to each give a short talk about their books. These are often busy events that have great sales!

The best way to get your book on our shelves…

Your book needs to fit our criteria. That means a professional looking cover, sold at a regular discount in Ingram (we do not know how to make that happen for you…we only know how to sell your book!), have the title on the spine, and have an ISBN and price on the back. It helps if your book is competitively priced and in a high interest area. We currently do not buy books directly from authors and we are only bringing in books from local King County authors.

If it fits those criteria and has sold well at Indie Author Night or we have been selling it on special request, we may decide to bring it in. Please email your book information info@brickandmortarbooks.com with “Indie Author” in the title, and indicate that you are local in your email. Due to the large volume of requests, we are not able to respond to each email though we do look at each request. If we decide to purchase your book through Ingram, we will let you know!
