This recipe is the most effective, efficient and tasty way to make the most perfect pear cobbler!

Oven Temperature: Prep Time: Cook Time: Serving size:
325 degrees Farenheit/165 degrees Celsius 15 minutes 60 minutes 7-8 people

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups sliced fresh pears
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon grated nutmeg
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 stick salted butter
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 cup low-fat milk

Steps:

  1. Heat the oven to 325 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Cut fresh pears into small thin slices to make four cups.
  3. Mix pears with 1/2 cup brown sugar.
  4. Take 2-quart baking dish, put butter in it and place in oven until butter is melted.
  5. Combine flour, baking soda, remaining sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg in a separate bowl. Mix well.
  6. In another bowl whisk egg and milk.
  7. Slowly combine egg and milk mixture with the dry ingredients mixture.
  8. Pour the mixture into the baking dish over the melted butter.
  9. Spoon pears over the batter. Do not mix.
  10. Place baking dish into oven. Bake for 60 minutes.
  11. After 60 minutes, remove cobbler dish and set it to cool.
  12. Serve with a scoop of Vanilla Ice Cream.
  13. Enjoy!!!
